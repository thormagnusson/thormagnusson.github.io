Thor Magnusson

A worker in rhythm, frequencies and intensities. Research and development in the areas of music and technology. I am a professor of future music in the Music Department at the University of Sussex.

Research interests include musical improvisation, new technologies for musical expression, live coding, musical notation and digital scores, artificial intelligence and computational creativity, programming education, and the philosophy of technology.
